Hey! Welcome to our page on Illegal Immigration.


Illegal immigration in the United States is the act of foreign nationals entering or remaining in America while violating the immigration policies, and not receiving authorization from the Federal Government.

One can be considered an illegal immigrant from the following:

  • Border crossing card violator- entering the United States through illegal entry.
  • Overstaying a visa- an individual who entered the United States by obtaining a visa but overstayed the time period.
